<h4><strong>International Diabetes &amp; Endocrinology Conference (IDEC) 2025 - 24 August 2025</strong></h4>
<p>ANAH Network President, Dr Troy Puar and EXCO member, Prof Leilani Mercado-Asis
were invited speakers at IDEC 2025 held in Myanmar. The conference was
co-organised by the Myanmar Society of Endocrinology and Metabolism and
Mynamar Diabetes Association.</p>
<p>Dr Puar presented on the topic "Comprehensive care of primary aldosteronism:
A multidisciplinary approach" while Prof Leilani shared on "Precision medicine
in pheochromocytoma and paraganglioma".</p>

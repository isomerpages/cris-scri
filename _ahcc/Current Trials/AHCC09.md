<p><strong>AHCC09</strong>
</p>
</th>
<th rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>A multi-national, double-blind, placebo-controlled, parallel randomized
arms, phase II trial to compare the safety and efficacy of SIRT-Y90 followed
by atezolizumab plus bevacizumab versus SIRT-Y90 followed by placebo in
patients with locally advanced hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) (STRATUM)
(NCT05377034)</p>

<p>Status</p>

EWALT 2022, APASL 2022, EASL 2024, APPLE 2024 and APPLE 2025.&nbsp;Published
in Liver Cancer. 2023 Oct 9;13(3):298-313. doi: 10.1159/000534513</p>

<p>Sim YK, Chong MC, Gandhi M, Pokharkar YM, Zhu Y, Shi L, Lequn L, Chen

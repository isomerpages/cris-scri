<h4>How do we ensure research remains relevant, reliable and ready to inform better decision-making in clinical studies?</h4>
<p>We are pleased to share that in collaboration with Cochrane Singapore
and the NUS Yong Loo Lin School of Medicine, we will be conducting our
annual Cochrane workshops, designed to equip clinical research professionals
and policy makers with the tools to produce high-quality research data
using the Cochrane method of systematic reviews and analysis.</p>

register!</p>

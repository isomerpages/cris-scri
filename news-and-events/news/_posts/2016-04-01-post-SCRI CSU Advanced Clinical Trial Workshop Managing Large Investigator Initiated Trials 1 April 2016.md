<p>SCRI has conducted an <strong>Advanced Clinical Trial Workshop </strong>on 1 April 2016 for the third consecutive year in collaboration with the <strong>Clinical Science Unit (CSU).</strong></p><p>This one-day practical workshop aims to help experienced investigators to manage their<br>Investigator Initiated Trials (IITs). Participants rotated through 3 practical sessions to discuss with SCRI Key Faculty members from the Epidemiology Department, <strong>CSO of SCRI, A/Prof Edwin Chan; Head of Epidemiology, Shi Luming; Charles Zheng, </strong>and from the Biostatistics Department, <strong>Head of Biostatistics, Mihir Gandhi; Dianne Bautista &amp; Lu Qingshu </strong>on their projects. The practical sessions touched on some of the issues such as project management, recruitment strategies &amp; study monitoring.</p><p>Collated feedbacks indicated over 90% of the participants were rated “Good” or “Excellent” for this workshop. SCRI is pleased to work with CSU to conduct this <strong>1-day </strong>Workshop and ensured that participants will be better equipped to continue their projects.</p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Resources/News 2016/33.jpg"></div><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Resources/News 2016/31.jpg"></div><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Resources/News 2016/32.jpg"></div><p></p>

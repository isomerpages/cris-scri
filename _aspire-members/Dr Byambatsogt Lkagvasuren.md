<h4>Dr Byambatsogt Lkagvasuren</h4>
<p>National Cardiovascular Center, Third State Central Hospital, Coronary
Care Unit
<br>
<br>Dr. Byambatsogt Lkhagvasuren is a leading figure in Mongolian Interventional
Cardiology. After graduating from medical school in 2014, he pursued a
Master of Science and cardiology training at Mongolian National University
of Medical Science in Mongolia. Since 2020, he has been serving at the
Third State Central Hospital as a cardiologist, where he advanced his expertise
in interventional cardiology through training in Korea, Korea University
Medical Center, Anam Hospital.</p>
<p>He has 5 years of experience in interventional cardiology and completed
a one-year advanced training program in valvular heart disease and peripheral
vascular interventions at Severance Cardiovascular center, Yonsei University
Hospital in Korea starting in 2023.</p>
<p>He is currently working in the Coronary Care Unit, where he performs elective
and emergency PCI procedures for patients with acute myocardial infarction
and those with chronic ischemic heart disease.</p>

<h4>Dr Mann Chandavimol</h4>
<p>Dr Mann Chandavimol specializes in Interventional Cardiology with special
interest in Coronary and Structural Heart Intervention. Today, he is also
the Director of Center of Excellence Heart and Director of Heart Institute
in Samitivej Sukhumvit Hospita</p>
<p>Dr Chandavimol graduated from Chulalongkorn University Medical School
in Thailand. He went on to do his fellowship in The University of British
Columbia where he was certified by The Royal college of Surgeons and Physicians
of Canada and by the American Board of Internal Medicine for Internal Medicine
and subsequently, in Cardiology. He then moved to Saint Paul’s Hospital,
Vancouver to complete his fellowship in Intervention Cardiology and subsequently,
Heart Failure and Heart Transplantation. Later, Dr Chandavimol returned
to serve Thailand as an Interventional Cardiologist at Ramathibodi Hospital
– Department of Medicine Mahidol University and Samitivej Sukhumvit Hospital.</p>
<p>Dr Chandavimil also leads the TAVR program at Ramathibodi Hospital. In
additional to coronary intervention, he also has special interest in TPVR,
valve in valve/ring procedures, Left atrial appendage occlusion, and other
transcatheter ACHD intervention. He has served as Scientific Chair and
Course Director/ Co-Director of many different conferences and workshops
through the years, including CSI Asia Pacific, the International Adult
Congenital Cardiology, Mahidol TAVI and Transcatheter Pulmonic Valve Implantation
Workshops. He has also served as a President the Cardiovascular Interventional
Association of Thailand (CIAT) since 2024 and a secretary of the Thai PCI
registry.</p>
<p>Dr Chandavimol has published over 45 papers in various journals including
Circulation AHA/ASA journals. He has also presented at various local, regional
and international conferences including American College of Cardiology
Congress (ACC), European Society of Cardiology (ESC), Transcatheter Cardiac
Intervention (TCT), PCR London valve and PCR Tokyo valve.</p>

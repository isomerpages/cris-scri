<h4>Prof Thusara Matthias</h4>
<p>Prof Thushara Matthias graduated with first class honors and 09 distinctions
from the Faculty of Medical Sciences, University of Sri Jayewardenepura
in 2009 and was placed first in the all-island merit list. She completed
her MD in Medicine in 2015 and was awarded the FRCP London in 2021 and
board-certified as a consultant physician in 2017. She trained in acute
medicine at Addenbrooke’s Hospital, Cambridge, UK, after which she joined
the Department of Medicine at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura and
works as a consultant physician attached to the &nbsp;Colombo South Teaching
Hospital.</p>
<p>Prof Matthias’s academic interests include cardiovascular disease (CVD),
lipids, especially familial hypercholesterolemia, and internal medicine.
Her research focuses on the identification and management of dyslipidemia,
integration of real-world data into clinical practice, and identifying
and addressing barriers in LMIC settings for CVD care.&nbsp; She has more
than 70 publications in peer-reviewed and indexed journals, including The
Lancet, PLOS One, and BMJ Open, and has presented her work at national
and international forums. She has also received research awards for her
scientific work, including the NRC Research Awards.</p>
<p>She presently functions as the National Lead Investigator of Sri Lanka
for EAS FSHC (The European Atherosclerosis Society Familial Hypercholesterolemia
Studies Collaboration).</p>

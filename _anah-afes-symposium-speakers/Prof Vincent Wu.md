<h3>Prof Vin-Cent Wu</h3>
<p><strong>MD, PhD</strong>
</p>
<p>Dept of Internal Medicine, Division of Nephrology</p>
<p>National Taiwan University Hospital</p>
<p>Group Director, Taiwan Primary Aldosteronism Investigation (TAIPAI)</p>
<h4>Profile</h4>
<p>Vin-Cent Wu, MD, PhD, is Professor of Medicine and Attending Physician
at National Taiwan University Hospital. He serves as Group Director of
the Taiwan Primary Aldosteronism Investigation (TAIPAI) and Chairman of
the Primary Aldosteronism Center at NTUH. Dr Wu’s research focuses on aldosteronism,
acute kidney injury, and cardiorenal metabolic diseases. He has published
over 380 peer-reviewed articles in journals such as <em>Hypertension</em>, <em>Nature Communications</em>, <em>JASN</em>,
and <em>JAMA Network Open</em>, and has been recognized among the world’s
top 2 % scientists lasted&nbsp;for 6 years&nbsp;by Stanford University.</p>

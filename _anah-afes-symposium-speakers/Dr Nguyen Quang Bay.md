<h4>Profile</h4>
<p>Dr. Nguyen Quang Bay, MD, PhD, serves as Head of Department of Endocrinology
at Bach Mai Hospital, Vietnam, and Chief of Division of Endocrinology in
the Department of Internal Medicine at Hanoi Medical University.</p>
<p>Between 2019 and 2023, Dr. Nguyen Quang Bay was consistently recognised
as The Best Lecturer at Hanoi Medical University. His clinical and academic
expertise was widely acknowledged through his participation as both speaker
and chairman at numerous national conferences and seminars. Notable engagements
included his role at the 3rd Asia-Pacific "Cardio-metabolic Education Forum"
held in Manila, Philippines, from 28-30 May 2010, and his poster presentation
at the 2021 Virtual American Endocrine Society's Annual Meeting.</p>
<p>Dr. Nguyen Quang Bay made substantial contributions to medical literature,
authoring 24 articles in Vietnamese medical journals and 4 publications
in international journals. He was featured as a special guest at the inaugural
InnovaTalk webinar in 2024, where he shared his expertise with the medical
community, particularly benefiting healthcare professionals in Vietnam.</p>
